Transcribe

Speech → text

Local Whisper transcription to SRT, WebVTT, plain text, or ELAN .eaf. Nothing is uploaded — audio never leaves your machine.

Archive Check

Format readiness

Inspect codecs and containers, flag them against DANS preferred formats, and convert to a preservation master.

A folder is scanned recursively (up to 500 media/image files).

Scrub Metadata

Strip hidden data

Find and remove EXIF, GPS, and embedded tags before you share. Images are rebuilt pixel-only; audio/video keep streams but drop all tags.
